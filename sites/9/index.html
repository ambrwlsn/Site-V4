Hey! I am Alison Hill Campbell, a freelance translator working from French and Portuguese into English.

About me

Established since 2008, I completed my MA in Translation + Linguistics from Westminster University, London.

I specialise in translation documents in the field of medicine and pharmaceuticals.

As a qualified member of the Institute of Translation and interpreting, I am able to certify my translations.

What I Do

Working for over 20 different clients since 2008, the types of texts I have translated include: Journal articles, Patient reports, Consent forms, Package leaflets, Summary of product characteristics (SMPC), Clinical trial documentation, Adverse event reports.

These cover the fields of Oncology, Cardiology, Orthopaedics, Immunology and my particular interest, Psychiatry.

I also do certificate translation – Diplomas, birth/marriage/death certificates, CVs.
